About Hog Rider

Hog Rider is your favorite hog riding rider. A performance artist in his own right, Hog Rider expresses his emotions through bold architectural critiques — usually by smashing towers at full speed atop a 600-pound hog. His signature “HOG RIDAAAAAAAA!” is less of a battle cry, more of a sonic statement on the chaos of modern elixir culture. From minimalist 2.6 cycle decks to heavy Hog EQ compositions, each deck is a canvas — and he? He’s the brush. Watch out. Art is about to hit your tower.
